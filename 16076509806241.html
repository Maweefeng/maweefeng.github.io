另类创建字典的方法

  • zip函数

    names = ['name','age','pay','job']
values = ['sam smith',40,3000,'software']
    
print(list(zip(names,values)))

  • 创建空字典

    names = ['name','age','pay','job']
record = dict.fromkeys(names,"?")
    
print(record)
